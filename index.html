Hello All!

My name is Wilma Suppers, I am a children entertainer, I have done this for more than 20 years in Franklin Auckland New Zealand. I Live in Waiuku, this gives me a great opportunity to get involved with all sorts of big events such as: Thomas The Tank Engine weekends, Steelmill Teddy bear picnic,Ford and Holden Event Pukekohe, Pukekohe Horse Racing, School Gala's , Wine and Food Festival etc etc. I have also done face-painting at the American Cup, and The Rugby World Cup in Auckland. My Children Birthday party's are available from Waiuku area towards Auckland area. The Birthday Party's can include all the activities, or some of the activities. I supply the tables/paint/art T-shirts/cardboard etc for the Plaster Art Activitie. The children can choose from different plaster ornaments to paint on the Birthday Party, and take it home afterwards. The prices can be discussed by phone or e-mail
